Atomic Projects

A collection of atomic, highly focused utilities for finance and productivity. Minimal tools built with vanilla JavaScript.

  • Ultradian Schedule

    Optimize focus and high performance with the Ultradian Schedule. Time management model based on 110-minute ultradian rhythms for circadian alignment and peak productivity.

    Generate, preview, and validate colors by adjusting Hue, Saturation, and Lightness (HSL). Ensures input adheres to 0â€“100 integers for web design and development.

  • Numerical Scale Generator

  • Price Optimizer

    Calculate the optimal selling price for e-commerce. Price Optimizer inverts fees, taxes, and costs (COGS, shipping, marketing) to ensure your target profit margin is met.

  • Profit Allocator

    Quickly allocate net profit into three fixed shares: Investments (1/3), Cash Reserves (1/6), and General Use (1/2). Calculate immediate business fund distribution.

  • Till Reconciler

    Quickly reconcile your cash register. The Till Reconciler calculates Expected Cash (Total) and compares it to the Final Change count to find the exact over/short Difference.

  • Staff Profit Split

    Calculate and distribute profit shares to staff tiers. Dynamically set Tax and Manager rates to determine the final Net Profit allocation for waiter staff.
