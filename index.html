Atomic Projects

A collection of small projects produced by Robson Niemeyer.

    Instantly generate and visually display random integers within a customizable range (min/max). Perfect for simulating dice rolls or quick randomization tasks.

    Calculate geometric and exponential sequences based on base value and ratio. Ideal for custom tuning, type scale design, and data modeling.
